Prophet Joseph Anderson

Prophet Joseph Anderson, the General Overseer & Founder of Agape Word Chapel International, which has its headquarters in Takoradi-Mpintsin, the Western Region of Ghana, has a chain of branches locally and internationally. His Ministry goes beyound the borders of Africa. He is a prolific Writer, Psalmist, Televangelist, Educationist, Marriage Counselor, and an Entrepreneur.
